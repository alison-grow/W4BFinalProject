Camping

There are several campgrounds within the park and many are close to spectacular views of the valley! Tents and trailers are welcome. Space is limited so reserve a campground now!

Attractions

The park is home to many natural attractions, such as Half Dome, El Capitan, several waterfalls, the Merced River, giant Sequoias, rare wildlife and more!

Activities

There are many things to do in Yosemite National Park, including hiking, fishing, horseback riding, rock climbing, boating, swimming, winter sports, and more!
