About Yosemite

All about your vacation here at the park.

l o d g i n g

There are several campgrounds within the park for you to bring your tent or trailer. Campground availability depends on the season. All but one campground closes for the winter, and reservations fill up fast in the summer. If camping is not your style, there are multiple hotels within the park to stay in! Cabin rentals and AirBnBs are available for anyone looking for private lodging.

f o o d

There are plenty of options for food inside Yosemite. The park is home to many restaurants and their menus range from fine dining to pizza and craft beer. Campers can bring food into the park for their stay, but it must be kept in a bear-proof container to prevent animal foraging. If you forget any food staples there is a grocery store within the park as well!

A c t i v i t i e s

There are tons of activities to do on your vacation to Yosemite. With miles of hiking trails to follow you will will get lost in the beauty of the park. Admire the unique wildlife such as the Sierra Nevada red fox, which can only be found in the Yosemite area. The Merced River allows for all kinds of water sports, like swimming, boating, and fishing. Sign up for a guided tour or visit the museum for an educational park experience.
